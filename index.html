About

Embedded Systems Engineer focused on low-level system architecture, with hands-on experience in designing runtime environments and hardware-interfacing drivers. Specialized in bootstrapping platforms from reset with custom linker scripts and startup code, and porting FreeRTOS and TCP/IP stacks over Ethernet MAC implementations. Strong expertise in USB 2.0 MSC and SD/eMMC driver development, with a focus on high-throughput data transfer and FATFS optimization. Demonstrates deep architectural understanding of cache coherency using SMMU, DMA-aware memory handling, and eliminating software cache maintenance via hardware snooping.
